ZimAlert – Child Safety & Protection Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

ZimAlert is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We provide a safe environment for all users, with strict policies against harmful content or behavior involving minors.

Prohibited Behavior

  • No sexual content involving minors
  • No grooming, trafficking, or exploitation
  • No sharing or requesting explicit images of minors
  • No predatory or abusive behavior

Monitoring & Enforcement

  • User reports are reviewed immediately
  • Harmful content is removed without delay
  • Accounts violating policies are permanently banned
  • Serious violations are reported to local authorities

Reporting Abuse

Users can report abuse directly in the app or by contacting our safety team:

  • Email: info@shifttechgs.com
  • In-app reporting tools are available for all users

Compliance

ZimAlert fully complies with:

  • Google Play Child Safety Policy
  • International and local child protection laws

We are dedicated to creating a safe platform for all users while protecting children from any form of abuse or exploitation.
